§ 01

One brand, two registers

The landing pages (Studio Pop) are loud — mesh gradients, marquees, big bold type, energy. The app is the opposite: quiet, dense, fast. The thread that ties them together is the tangerine brand color and the shared type family. When a creator clicks "Start launching" on a vibrant landing page, they cross into this calmer surface — same brand, different job.

CREATOR APP

Comfortable. Confident. Clean.

Marketplace browse, Brand Assets, Design Studio. Comfortable spacing, larger imagery, the tangerine accent on primary actions. Feels premium without shouting.

  • 24px card padding · generous whitespace
  • Tangerine glow on the one primary CTA per view
  • Bricolage for the occasional welcome/empty-state moment
PARTNER + ADMIN

Dense. Operational. No nonsense.

Order queues, verification, dashboards. Compact spacing, data tables, fewer animations. Tangerine appears only on the action that matters — accept, ship, approve.

  • 16px card padding · tabular numbers
  • Tangerine reserved for the row's primary action
  • Optional dark mode default for long sessions

The tangerine rule

Tangerine-500 on white is only 3.13:1 — fine as a button fill (white text on it hits 4.61:1) but it fails as body text. For tangerine text on light surfaces, always step to tangerine-700 (#C2410C, 5.94:1). This is the one trap in the palette.

Beyond contrast

  • Focus rings: 3px tangerine-500/18 on all interactive elements
  • Touch targets ≥ 44×44px
  • prefers-reduced-motion respected globally
  • Color never alone — paired with icon or text
  • Semantic HTML + visible labels on every input
