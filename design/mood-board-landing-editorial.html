From the editor · Pavel Georgiev

A platform built for the moment before the launch.

Most platforms meet you on the day your brand goes live. iLaunchify meets you on the day you decide it should exist. We built the connective tissue — manufacturers, label printers, co-packers, warehouses — into a single surface so creators can spend less time coordinating and more time imagining.

This issue features three creators who took an idea from notebook to shelf in under thirty days. Their stories share one thread: a refusal to wait for an industry that wasn't built for them.

Pavel Georgiev Founder & Editor
In this issue

Three creators, three launches, thirty days.

Each story below began as an idea in a notebook. Each one is now on a shelf or in a fulfillment center, ready to ship.

01
Wellness · Adaptogens

Sarah Chen's quiet morning ritual.

A clinical psychologist by training, Sarah spent two years formulating an adaptogenic blend designed for the half-hour before her patients arrived. The result — three SKUs, FDA-compliant labels, partnership with a Vermont co-packer — went from spreadsheet to first batch in twenty-six days.

Sarah Chen · Founder, Hush Wellness · 240k followers
02
Culinary · Cold-pressed

Maya Reyes is rewriting the condiment aisle.

A line of three sauces — coconut-lime, mango-habanero, smoked carrot — designed for the kind of dinner you take a picture of. Maya's launch hit a 4,000-unit pre-order ceiling in eighteen days. Her partner network now spans three regions.

Maya Reyes · Founder, Reyes Pantry · 480k followers
03
Beverage · Functional

Devon Aoki on what after-hours actually means.

An RTD botanical aperitif designed for the people who want the ritual without the alcohol. Devon built the brand alongside her fitness platform, launching across three states with a single click in iLaunchify's marketplace.

Devon Aoki · Founder, Hour & Half · 1.1m followers

From the production floor

A printer's case for fewer middlemen.

"Twenty years ago, taking on a small creator launch meant six emails, three sample reviews, and a calendar of phone tag. iLaunchify replaces all of that with a queue I can actually run my floor against."

The platform's queue tells his pressmen what's next; the platform's payment terms tell his bookkeeping when to expect funds.
