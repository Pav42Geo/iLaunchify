CLAUDE'S RECOMMENDATION

Eucalyptus Stone — best brand fit for iLaunchify.

It evolves your Suplifuls heritage naturally (green family) without copying its brightness. It hits the F&B-premium target your personas actually buy from (Sweetgreen, Brightland, Aplós). It gives partners the most credible-yet-warm surface to live in for daily work. And it's maximally distinct from Claude — different hue family, different serif, slightly cooler canvas. Terra remains a worthy fallback if you want maximum editorial warmth. Cherry is the right pick only if creator-aspiration outweighs partner-gravitas entirely, which it doesn't for a two-sided platform.
